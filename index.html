About

My name is Ryan Timmons, and I have a passion for developing software and web applications, as well as tackling new challenges.

I use a solid mix of languages and modern utilities in order to create functional, user-friendly websites and apps.

My developer's toolkit includes client-side essentials like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, back-end MVC frameworks like Ruby on Rails and Spring MVC, database management systems including SQLite and MySQL, and other useful libraries/frameworks such as jQuery, CoffeeScript, Sass, Bootstrap, and AngularJS.

I have rounded out my skillset as a developer through the use of Git as a version control system, the study of Agile project methodologies, and the development of critical debugging and research skills.

I have also ensured that the code I produce is stable and durable by adhering to test-driven development and behavior-driven development principles supported by the use of JUnit, Selenium, RSpec, and Capybara.
