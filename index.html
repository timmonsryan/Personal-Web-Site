About

My name is Ryan, and I develop web applications.

I use a variety of languages and cutting-edge utilities to create functional, user-friendly websites and apps.

My developer's toolkit includes client-side essentials like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, back-end MVC frameworks like Ruby on Rails, database management systems including SQLite and PostgreSQL, and other useful libraries/frameworks such as jQuery, CoffeeScript, Sass, Bootstrap, and AngularJS.

I have rounded out my skillset as a developer through the use of Git as a version control system, the study of Agile project methodologies, and the development of critical debugging and research skills.

I have also ensured that the code I produce is stable and durable by adhering to test-driven development and behavior-driven development principles supported by the use of RSpec, Capybara, and a bit of Cucumber.
